Broken pieces

I forgot, you didn’t remind, laboriously complained We shouted, and named and continually blamed— ...I threw the plate on the floor Once beautiful piece of glassware, fallen, lying just like us Apart and broken, thinking we boarded the wrong bus— ...Tension was cut, we both bleed Crouched at the edge of the sofa, staring into the abyss Your cheeks wet, red eyes, making the cosmos freeze— ...Silence has captured the room I say I love you, “I don’t think so” you carelessly rebutted “I don’t know what got over me”, overwhelmed, I quitted— ...Love seems to have no space I pick up the pieces, tears rolling, cut myself, I bleed further We are both kneeling, my hand in yours, I pray beyond the ether— ...Love split in two, but only one lost Eyes break, feelings revoked, and even the love, I trembled Only I am kneeling, your cheeks dried, you know I crumbled— ...You left and thunder rumbled.
