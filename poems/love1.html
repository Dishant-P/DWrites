One more time

Even a short walk to the town's end Seems like burying myself alive Every step feels like a step in quicksand The ground pulling me in Or maybe its just you. Those lonely winter nights Shivering in pain Very warmth from my blood seems washed away Give me the warmth of your breath Under those cool set of sheets, one more time. I wanna row in the lake, where the lilies grow To watch the water coming to life and catch its breath And hear it bubbling up, as we dip the wood tearing it open One stroke at a time I wanna listen to your heartbeat once again Lying down on your chest Ear lying on your soul And just let it speak every one of your secrets to me Just one more time. I wanna listen to one more good book Lying in your lap and flowing in the sweet narration of your voice Feeling the silhouette of yours, on my face Caused by the light stroke of sun from the tree above us Even now when you are not here I want us to breathe again together I wanna feel the comfort of your lips On mine, on the night bridge One more time. Everyday I see that jar Wishing to open it once more and let my hand enter that ball of life Even though they are ashes I would be able to touch you one more time Just one. More. Time.
