Little less sad

The static crackling of the old light Endless noise from outside and more from within The sense of complete chaos at the door All of the wrong, all of the dark The constant motion of fatigue All going on inside of me, and more And then not just me, but the entire world ruptures Not in pieces, but from within itself Isolating even the tide from the shore And it all stops. Just for that one moment, we all come to a standstill It was time for me to part myself from things that left me sore It was time to purge. I take out my weapon Guilt, pity, hurt, stress, I let it all flow out As gently as possible, as aggressively as I can, and then some more And now these feelings have got inked, I am glad Not on a person, but this old yellow piece of paper I forgot how it felt to scream without disturbing the ripple’s core Well, it’s done. I drop the paper and go out I let the air fill my lungs all to its bottom And let it all out just to realize that ‘yes! I have some space indoor’ I can now let the droplets of cold water kiss my toes I can now feel the sun on my face for as long as I want to I can now tell myself, it's okay to cry and to adore Cause I now have the strength, I now have space I now can tell myself, I am a little less sad than before
