Home

When lost, tired, or scared With trembling thoughts or weeping eyes With thousands of thoughts crawling There’s one which never stays away ‘Its okay to miss your home today’ (Few months ago) Exhilarated by the newly signed contract The declaration of freedom Going away, to get closer to my dreams The last wave and wink and cry Not turning around anymore, goodbye Thoughts about the things left behind Memories were still with me The smell of the tea in the morning With first rain and sitting under the shade In the garden where the creaking swing was laid Fresh faces, fresh place, fresh freedom But I won’t lie, in city I do miss the fresh air No one to take care, nor to scold or nag Loved it all but still felt a little emptiness Like eating desired ice cream, but void of sweetness Facing the challenges and being bold And some few other things my dad told I ignore all of that, cause I was me now I take the day as I wish and fell hard I get up, no one standing by to hold my card I drag my body to the mess window Forgetting to take money, I come empty handed To the room, I open the cupboard I take out mumma’s milk cake and with that bite Everything rushed out my eyes, not a lovely sight I was lost, tired, and scared With trembling thoughts and weeping eyes With thousands of thoughts crawling There’s one which did not stay away ‘Its okay to miss your home today’ I do miss it. I do miss my home today
