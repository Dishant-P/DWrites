About the conversations

Welcome to Conversations with you; without you. It is not just a poem diary but also a window into my life or out of yours. These conversations provide an escape from reality or sometimes bring us closer to it. The conversation entries are not just for me, but maybe even for you. When you feel out of breath or need to close your eyes and smile, I would know. And I would be grateful to have had a chance to drive you down memory lane.

Each poem has its own diction rule. It is something that I have appended to make it easier for you to feel the feeling that I wanted to lock in those words. Each poem would have, at its end, the magic recipe, the interpretation of mine. You may want to skip it if you like to keep that poetry as yours; it's any way up for grabs.

Much love,

Coffee?

Well, as with any writer, I love coffee (no pun or stereotype intended, I like tea too). So, if you are in the city and have an interesting conversation, just grab a cup and ping me on the given two channels. I would love to meet if time permits (or the cosmos, it depends). Cheers.
