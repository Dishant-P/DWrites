About the conversations

Welcome to the conversations. This is not just a poem diary but also a window into my life or out of yours. Conversations provide the escape from reality and talk about the things I wished to, with you. The conversations are not just for me, but maybe even for you. When you feel out of breath or need to just close your eyes and smile, I would know. And I would be grateful to have had a chance to drive you down the memory (or the imaginary) lane.

Each poem has its own diction rule. It is something that I have appended in order to make it easier for you to feel the feeling that I wanted to lock in those words. Each poem would have, at its end the magic reciepe, the interpretation of mine. You may wanna skip it if you want to keep that poetry as yours, its anyway up for grabs. Much love,
